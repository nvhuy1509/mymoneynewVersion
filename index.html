What is

MyMoney.Credit?

My Money Credit offers a comprehensive range of financial solutions that prioritize privacy and security. We bridge the gap between traditional finance and decentralized finance (DeFi) platforms, allowing our users to benefit from the best of both worlds.
With our privacy-and-security focused solutions, you can confidently manage your finances without compromising your personal information. Our advanced solutions give you greater control, efficiency, security, and flexibility over your money. Experience the future of finance with us.

Flexible financial leverage

Users can use assets such as BTC, ETH, stocks, or bonds as collateral to borrow cash from My Money Credit without selling their assets.

Global cash flow
Investors can easily invest in financial products in other countries without the hassles of liquidating assets and transferring money across borders.
Tax exemption
Users can borrow cash or stablecoin for utilities without capital gains taxes on their collateral.
Uncollateralized microloans
My Money Credit offers unsecured small loans with quick loan approvals due to the use of automatic credit ratings and rating algorithms.
