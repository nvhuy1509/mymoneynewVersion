  • Remittance and USDC/VND USDT/VNDTransactions
    Our app offers a solution for users to save on remittance costs by converting USD to USDC or USDT at a 1:1 rate and trading the USDC/VND and USDT/VND pairs at a minimal expense or no cost on MMC.
  • StablecoinPremium
    The stablecoin premium in Vietnam refers to the situation where the market value of USDT and USDC is higher than USD when converted to VND, leading to higher costs for acquiring stablecoins in Vietnam, but presenting arbitrage opportunities for investors with access to international money markets.
  • MMCToken
    MMC token is an utility token issued on Ethereum’s Layer 2 by My Money Credit, serving as collateral for loans, interest payment, platform profitability, equity representation, fee distribution, auto buyback, and governance.
  • MMC Wallet
    My Money Credit offers an e-wallet to store, mortgage, and lend coins/tokens, with the ability to collateralize various assets for loans at different Loan-to-Value ratios, and with profitable interest rates for deposited assets and low interest rates for secured loans.
