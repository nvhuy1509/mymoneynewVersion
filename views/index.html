Bridge the Gap Between Traditional Finance and Decentralized Finance for a Smarter, More Inclusive Financial Future

Maximize your assets with borrowing, investing, and exchanging both fiat and cryptocurrency. Grow your account anytime, anywhere with My Money Credit.

  • Visions

    Create a decentralized financial ecosystem that bridges traditional and decentralized finances with seamless borrowing, investing, and exchange services for users worldwide.

  • Missions

    My Money Credit is committed to providing a user-friendly, innovative, and integrated platform that leverages big data and blockchain technology to deliver efficient and secure borrowing, lending, investing, and exchange services. Our mission is to enable our clients to maximize their financial potential while maintaining the highest standards of compliance, risk management, and customer service.

Why choose MY MONEY CREDIT

  • Global cash flow Investors can easily invest in financial products in other countries without the hassles of liquidating assets and transferring money across borders.
  • Tax exemption Users can borrow cash or stablecoin for utilities without capital gains taxes on their collateral.
  • Uncollateralized microloans My Money Credit offers unsecured small loans with quick loan approvals due to the use of automatic credit ratings and rating algorithms.
  • Flexible financial leverage Users can use assets such as BTC, ETH, stocks, or bonds as collateral to borrow cash from My Money Credit without selling their assets.
  • MMC TOKEN The backbone of My Money Credit, creating solid trust value for users while granting them many exclusive benefits within the platform
