MMC Wallet
The ultimate solution for convenient, private, and secure transactions

Access to TradFi and DeFi

on Your Phone

Enhanced Privacy
MMC Wallet offers a high level of privacy through features like Stealth Address and Shield Transaction, ensuring that wallet addresses and transaction details remain hidden from the public blockchain. This provides users with greater anonymity and confidentiality
Secure & Confidential Transactions
With Multi-Party Computation (MPC) technology, MMC Wallet divides the seed phrase among various devices, preventing any single device from having access to the complete seed phrase. This enhances security and reduces the risk of unauthorized access
ETH-less Transactions
MMC Wallet allows users to perform transactions without the need for ETH, eliminating the need to transfer ETH to the wallet and further enhancing privacy by reducing connections between wallets
Native Fiat Gateway
Users can conveniently access a native fiat gateway within MMC Wallet, facilitating seamless conversion between cryptocurrencies and fiat currencies, enhancing flexibility in managing funds
Efficient Features
MMC Wallet integrates various efficient features, such as Private OTC P2P trading, CCTP (Cross-Chain Transfer Protocol), batch transactions, etc. These features simplifies transactions and grants users instant access to both TradFi and DeFi at their fingertips
